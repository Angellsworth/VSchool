About Us

Mario's Pest Control is a small Italian-American/family-owned business that began in the summer of 1983. Mario and his brother Luigi provide quality pest control at competitive rates to help solve all your pest needs. You can count on us to jump in and on and over and under to squash your pest problems. We are mostly licensed and pretty much insured and try really hard to follow all state and federal guidelines, but when we got to clear the way to the castle we do whatever it takes. Staying alive is our number one priority while providing a pest free environment. For very remote and tough to get to jobs we bring our Boss Princess Peach (you should see her drive).

Thank you for the opportunity and we look forward to working with you.
